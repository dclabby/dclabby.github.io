About



Sheep's Head Peninsula, West Cork, September 2020

I'm Darragh Clabby from Dublin, Ireland. I studied as a mechanical engineer and completed a PhD at Queen's University Belfast, before embarking on a career in marine renewable energy. I've been fortunate enough to have worked with many amazing people as part of companies developing solutions to extremely challenging problems, specifically: harnessing the energy contained ocean waves and currents.

A large part of my work of my work has involved development of numerical simulation tools, probabilistic models & statistical analysis, and analysis of data from operating machines & meteorological instruments. This work has given me a great base to explore the fascinating fields of software development, data science, and Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning.
